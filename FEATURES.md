## üèÜ Milestone Tracker

### 5 Training Phases with Specific Milestones

**Foundation Phase (7 milestones)**
- Sits on command 95%
- Downs on command 95%
- Stays 2 minutes at 6 feet
- Recalls from 20 feet
- Heels for 5 minutes
- Leaves items when cued
- Socialized with 30+ people

**Advanced Obedience (6 milestones)**
- Commands in 5 environments
- 5-minute stays with distractions
- Ignores food on ground
- Ignores dogs at 10 feet
- Off-leash reliability
- Socialized with 60+ people, 20+ dogs

**Task Training (6 milestones)**
- Task #1 at 90% reliability
- Task #2 at 90% reliability
- Task #3 at 90% reliability
- Tasks work in different environments
- Tasks work with distractions
- Tasks performed independently

**Public Access (9 milestones)**
- Controlled entry/exit
- Perfect heel in public
- 6-foot sit-stay in store
- 3-minute down-stay with distractions
- Ignores other dogs in public
- Ignores food distractions
- Calm in restaurant 30 minutes
- Calm in elevator/confined spaces
- 2-hour public outing success

**Full Service Dog Status (5 milestones)**
- Passes complete PAT
- All tasks 95%+ reliable
- Calm during 4-hour outing
- Handles unexpected situations
- Works reliably all environments
