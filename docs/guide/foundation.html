Phase 1: Foundation Obedience (Weeks 1-8)

ğŸ¯ Objective: Establish reliable basic commands in low-distraction environments

Essential Commands

1. Name Recognition

Foundation

Goal: Gypsy turns attention to you within 1 second of hearing her name

Training Method
  1. Say her name once in a happy, upbeat tone
  2. The instant she looks at you, mark with "Yes!"
  3. Reward immediately with treat and praise
  4. Practice 20-30 repetitions daily in 3-5 minute sessions
Pro Tips for Pitbulls
  • Use high-value treats - pitbulls are very food motivated
  • Keep sessions short and fun - their enthusiasm is high
  • Never repeat her name - say it once and wait
  • Practice in different rooms to generalize

2. Sit

Foundation

Goal: Sits within 2 seconds on verbal cue or hand signal

Training Method
  1. Hold treat at Gypsy's nose level
  2. Slowly move treat up and back over her head
  3. As her bottom touches the ground, mark "Yes!"
  4. Reward immediately
  5. Add verbal cue "Sit" once she's reliably following the lure

Training Principles for Pitbulls

ğŸ¯ Positive Reinforcement Protocol

  • Mark the Behavior: Use clicker or "Yes!" at exact moment of correct behavior
  • Reward Immediately: Within 1-2 seconds of behavior
  • High-Value Rewards: Small, soft treats Gypsy loves (chicken, cheese, hot dogs)
  • Variable Reinforcement: Once learned, reward randomly to maintain reliability
