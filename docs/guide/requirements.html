Service Dog Requirements & Standards

ADA Task Requirements

Service dogs must perform specific tasks that:

  1. Directly relate to your disability
  2. Are actively trained behaviors (not natural behaviors)
  3. Mitigate specific disability-related limitations
  4. Can be demonstrated if challenged

Examples of Valid Tasks for Veterans:

  • Nightmare Interruption: Tactile stimulation to wake from nightmares
  • Room Clearing: Safety checks before entering spaces
  • Crowd Control: Space buffering in crowded areas
  • Panic Attack Interruption: Deep pressure therapy during episodes
  • Medication Reminders: Alerting at scheduled times
  • Balance Support: Bracing for mobility assistance
